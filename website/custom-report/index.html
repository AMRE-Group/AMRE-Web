FAQ

Common questions.

What's in the report?

Median list price, price per square foot, days on market, inventory, price-cut share, market segments by tier, and the Market Action Index — plus a short written read on what it means for your move.

Is it really free?

Yes — no fee, no obligation. It's how we start conversations with buyers and sellers who like to decide with data.

Can I get a report for a specific property?

Yes. If you include an address, we'll build the report around your street and property type, including recent comparable sales.

How current is the data?

Reports are built from weekly market feeds, so figures reflect the current week — not a quarterly or monthly lag.
