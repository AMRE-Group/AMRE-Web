The Smart Move

Why a Duplex Changes
Everything

This is the single highest-leverage decision you can make at your price point. Here's why Michael recommends it — and he's done it more than once.

The Case For

A duplex at your budget means you're not just buying a home — you're buying a cash-flowing investment that pays you back every single month.

  • Collect $2,000–$3,000/mo from the second unit
  • Reduces your effective mortgage by 30–40%
  • No HOA. No shared walls with strangers. No restrictions.
  • Rent both units if you travel or rent out rooms
  • Entire property appreciates — you own twice the real estate
  • Tax benefits: depreciation + expense deductions on rental unit
  • Live in one unit for 2 years → rent both if you ever relocate

What to Know

Duplexes require more due diligence upfront — but that's what you have us for. Here's what we check on every one.

  • Tenant status: occupied or vacant? Rent control applies in LA
  • Current rent vs market rate — key to real yield math
  • Separate utility meters? (big deal for economics)
  • Permits: both units permitted and legal?
  • Deferred maintenance on the second unit
  • Zoning confirmation: can you ADU or add a unit later?
  • West Adams, Highland Park, Pasadena = best duplex hunting grounds
