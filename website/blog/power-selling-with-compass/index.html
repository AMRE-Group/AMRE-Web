What is Compass Concierge?

Compass Concierge is a game-changer for homeowners. You can apply for up to $40,000 in home improvements for just a $750 flat fee on homes listed up to $4 million. Need more? The program covers up to $75,000 for homes between $4 million and $6 million, with transparent interest rates and fees. No out-of-pocket expenses, no juggling contractors — Compass helps manage everything. You simply repay when your home sells, or 12 months after approval, whichever comes first. Homes prepared this way consistently sell faster and for higher prices.

Which LA homes benefit most?

Concierge delivers the biggest return on homes that show poorly relative to their potential — dated finishes, deferred paint, no staging, tired curb appeal. That's exactly where targeted improvements move buyer perception and sale price the most. The judgment call — which improvements actually return more than they cost — is where an experienced local agent earns their keep.
