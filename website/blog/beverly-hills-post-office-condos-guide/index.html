Beverly Hills Post Office: the 90210 address that's actually in LA.

BHPO carries one of the most famous ZIP codes in the world — 90210 — but it's technically in Los Angeles, not Beverly Hills. Here's what BHPO really is, what condos and homes cost in 2026, and what separates it from its famous neighbor below.

What BHPO actually is.

Beverly Hills Post Office — BHPO, or Beverly Crest — is a hillside neighborhood in the City of Los Angeles that sits just north of Beverly Hills city limits. It runs roughly from Mulholland Drive down to a few blocks above Sunset, between Benedict Canyon Drive and Coldwater Canyon. The area has carried the 90210 ZIP code since the Beverly Hills post office began serving deliveries there, but that mailing convenience created a famous quirk: your address reads Beverly Hills, CA 90210 while your city government, police, fire department, and schools are all Los Angeles.

Same ZIP, different cities

  • BHPO / Beverly Crest — City of Los Angeles, LAUSD schools, LA city services; 90210 mailing address; lower price per square foot at the entry level.
  • Beverly Hills proper — independent city, Beverly Hills Unified schools, Beverly Hills police & fire; higher per-square-foot pricing.

What it costs to buy in BHPO.

BHPO spans a wide price range. The major gated communities — Beverly Park, Mulholland Estates, The Summit, Beverly Ridge Estates — are among the most expensive addresses in the country, with estates trading well above $10 million and the 90210-wide average sale price running around $6–7 million in recent months. But BHPO is not monolithic. Lower BHPO, closer to Sunset, offers entry-level hillside inventory that can trade at or below $1,200 per square foot — meaningfully less than comparable addresses inside Beverly Hills proper.

Condos and townhomes in BHPO.

BHPO is overwhelmingly a single-family, estate-and-gated-community market. Condos and townhomes exist but are rare — inventory data shows only one or two at any given time across the broader BHPO area. When they do appear they tend to sit within smaller condo complexes or HOA communities near the lower, more accessible edge of the neighborhood, typically priced well below the estate range. For buyers specifically looking for a condo with a 90210 BHPO address, patience and market timing matter more than in any other segment we track.

The prestige is real — but the schools are LAUSD, the services are LA city, and entry-level pricing reflects that. BHPO is not a substitute for a Beverly Hills proper address if city schools are your priority.

What the gated communities actually offer.

The four major BHPO gated communities each have their own character. Dues, rules, security levels, and minimum lot sizes vary significantly between them — each warrants individual diligence.

The architect-agent edge on hillside property.

BHPO is canyon and hillside real estate — the category where the difference between an architect-trained eye and a generalist matters most. Hillside construction in Los Angeles involves retaining walls, drainage systems, soils reports, geotechnical conditions, and foundation systems that a standard walkthrough simply won't surface. A view that sells a property can also mean a foundation that cost a fortune to build — and may require more to maintain.
