Frequently asked questions.

How much do I need to buy a $599K condo in Los Angeles?

With 20% down you'd bring roughly $119,800 — less if a credit applies (here, $99,800 after a $20,000 credit) — plus closing costs, financing about $479,200 on a 30-year fixed loan.

What does the monthly payment include?

The ~$4,472/mo estimate covers principal and interest ($3,199), property tax ($624), insurance (~$100), and the $549 HOA fee.

Is Mid-City a good area to buy?

It's one of LA's fastest-evolving corridors — see our full Mid-City neighborhood guide for schools, walkability, and market data.
