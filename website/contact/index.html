FAQ

Common questions.

How quickly will you respond if I send a message?

We respond to every message personally within a few hours during business days. After-hours messages typically get a reply by 9am the next business morning.

Can I just have an exploratory conversation before committing to anything?

Absolutely. The first call or coffee is on us — no obligation, no pressure. We'd rather have a useful 30-minute conversation about your goals than a transactional one about ours.

Do you work with first-time buyers or only luxury / investment clients?

Both. We represent first-time owner-occupant buyers, seasoned investors, sellers preparing for retirement, and architectural-significant transactions. Every client gets the same level of service regardless of price point.

How do I get a free home valuation?

Submit the contact form below or email us directly. We'll schedule a brief walkthrough (in-person or virtual) and follow up with a comparative market analysis tailored to your home and neighborhood.

Can we meet in person, or is everything done remotely?

Both options work. Our office is at Compass Beverly Hills (9454 Wilshire Blvd, Suite 100). We're equally happy to meet at your home, on-site at a property, or over Zoom — whichever fits your schedule.
