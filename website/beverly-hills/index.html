Common Questions

Beverly Hills, answered.

What areas make up Beverly Hills?

The flats south of Sunset, the foothills north of Sunset, Trousdale Estates, and the Beverly Hills Post Office (BHPO) area in the canyons.

Is Beverly Hills a good investment?

Beverly Hills real estate has historically held value through cycles thanks to scarcity, schools, and global demand. We underwrite each property individually.

Does AMRE represent both buyers and sellers here?

Yes — buyers, sellers, and investors, with Compass Private Exclusive access and an architect-trained eye for value.
