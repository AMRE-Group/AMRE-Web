About Michael

A different lens
on Los Angeles.

Michael Abraham is a Los Angeles real estate professional — a licensed Realtor®, licensed architect, and active member of the National Association of Realtors®. Based at Compass Beverly Hills, Michael represents sellers, buyers, and investors across Beverly Hills, Bel Air, Brentwood, Santa Monica, Malibu, Studio City, Culver City, West Adams, and the broader LA Westside.

Michael's architectural training shapes everything he brings to a transaction. He reads floor plans the way most people read a paragraph — instinctively, in context — and pairs that with over a decade of personal investing in Los Angeles multifamily real estate. The result is an advisor who can speak fluently to a first-time buyer evaluating their first condo and to a sophisticated 1031-exchange investor evaluating a value-add fourplex.

For sellers, that translates to pricing discipline, renovation guidance that moves the appraisal needle, and a marketing playbook engineered around Compass's three-phase launch strategy. For buyers, it means cold-eyed property analysis, clear-eyed contingency strategy, and a partner who has personally signed for, renovated, and operated Los Angeles real estate — not just sold it.

Michael partners with Ania De Pourbaix as AMRE Real Estate Group at Compass. Two senior agents are actively engaged on every transaction. Clients don't get handed off — they get two principals, one playbook, and the full Compass platform behind every deal.

What Michael Does

Six lanes.
One standard.

Michael's practice is intentionally focused. Every client gets the same advisory depth — whether the deal is a starter duplex or an eight-figure architectural showcase.

Architecturally Significant Homes

Mid-century, modernist, post-and-beam, and contemporary properties where reading the structure is half the deal. Trained eye on circulation, light, structure, and renovation upside.

Multifamily Investment

Duplexes through 20-unit value-add. Underwriting that respects rent control, tenant law, deferred maintenance, and the actual cost to operate — not just the broker's pro forma.

1031 Exchanges

Identification windows, replacement-property analysis, qualified-intermediary coordination, boot and basis modeling. Tight timelines handled with a checklist, not a prayer.

Seller Advisory

Compass three-phase launch strategy: private exclusives, Coming Soon, full MLS. Pricing discipline, pre-list improvements with measurable ROI, and a marketing engine built for the LA buyer.

Buyer Representation

Off-market sourcing through Compass Private Exclusives, due-diligence frameworks borrowed from architecture practice, and contingency strategies that protect your deposit.

Value-Add & Renovation

Architect-led analysis of what to change, what to leave, and what to permit. Renovation budgets that pencil, scope that pencils harder, and timelines that don't quietly slip a year.
