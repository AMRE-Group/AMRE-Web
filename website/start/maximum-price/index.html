Common Questions

What people actually want to know.

Direct answers, no fluff.

Why is this free? What's the catch?+
No catch. We'd rather earn your business by giving you something genuinely valuable up front than by cold-calling you. If we earn your trust, hopefully you'll think of us when you're ready to list. If not, you still keep a real report. Fair trade.
Will you pressure me to list with you?+
No. We will not call, email, or contact you again about listing unless you specifically ask us to. We send the report, we step back, you decide. Sales pressure damages the brand we've spent 25 years building — it's not worth it to us, ever.
What if I'm not selling for a year — or three?+
Even better. Knowing what your home is worth today changes how you plan everything else — refinances, renovations, education funding, retirement timing. A surprising number of our clients ask for a report years before they sell. Some never do. That's fine.
Is this AI-generated or done by a junior?+
Neither. Every report is personally written by Michael (Licensed Architect, DRE #02242095) and Ania (DRE #01891438). That's the entire point — we don't sacrifice depth for scale, ever.
How accurate are these vs. Zillow?+
Zillow uses an algorithm that doesn't know your home, your block, or which side of the street gets the canyon view. We look at the actual property, the actual recent sales, the actual current competition, and our actual buyer pipeline. Our valuations typically land within 2–3% of final sale price.
