What We Watch On The Westside

Themes across the corridor.

01

Inventory stays tight

Good homes on the Westside remain scarce relative to the buyers who want them. Limited credible alternatives keep well-positioned listings in demand across the corridor.

02

Coast and land carry a premium

Proximity to the water and the underlying value of the land drive pricing on the Westside as much as the house itself — and that premium behaves differently city to city.

03

Discipline wins the first two weeks

The opening fortnight sets the tone. Homes priced with discipline out of the gate tend to attract the strongest buyers; chasing the market down rarely recovers the difference.

04

Presentation and reach decide the buyer

On the Westside the right buyer is often global. Preparation, staging and the breadth of a home's marketing reach do more to determine the outcome than square footage alone.

Westside FAQ

The corridor, explained.

Which Westside neighborhoods does the report cover?

The report tracks the full Westside corridor — Santa Monica, Venice and Culver City, plus Brentwood, Mar Vista, Marina del Rey and Pacific Palisades. We publish per-city reports for the first three and prepare the others on request for your specific street.

Is it a buyer's or seller's market on the Westside right now?

It depends on the city and the price point — the Westside is not one market. Each neighborhood carries its own Market Action reading, so Santa Monica, Venice and Culver City can lean in different directions at the same time. We read each corridor city on its own terms rather than quoting a single number for the whole Westside.

How fast do homes sell on the Westside?

It varies widely by neighborhood, price and presentation. Correctly priced, well-presented homes tend to find their buyer in the first two weeks, while coastal and land-value properties can take longer. Pace is a function of positioning, not just the calendar.

How do I get a report for my specific street?

Request a custom report and we will prepare a read tailored to your block — pricing, comparable activity and how your home would position today, across any Westside neighborhood including Brentwood, Mar Vista, Marina del Rey and Pacific Palisades.
