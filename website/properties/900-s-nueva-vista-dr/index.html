The Residence

A refreshed mid-century retreat where the desert light, the mountains, and the water meet.

Set on a quiet street in the coveted South End, 900 South Nueva Vista is a beautifully styled 1976 pool home — brand-new flooring, hardware, and designer interiors throughout, with an active short-term-rental permit. Offered turnkey, with furnishings optional, and ready from the moment you arrive.

The Grounds

An afternoon that never ends

A brand-new pool and spa anchor a backyard built for the Palm Springs sun — sun deck, fire pit, and a built-in BBQ framed by uninterrupted San Jacinto mountain views.

Mornings begin on the water; evenings drift around the fire long after the desert cools.

Available optionally furnished — move in with every room in place, or make the space entirely your own.
