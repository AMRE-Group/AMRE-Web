What's special

Set behind gates on an expansive 18,962-square-foot lot, this designer-crafted Mid-Century ranch offers an exceptional blend of privacy, scale, and effortless California living in one of Pacific Palisades' most coveted locations. Surrounded by mature landscaping and lush privacy hedging, the home unfolds as a tranquil single-level retreat where sophisticated interiors seamlessly connect to resort-inspired outdoor spaces.

Warm wood floors, soaring vaulted ceilings, sculptural lighting, and walls of glass create a light-filled atmosphere. An inviting foyer draws the eye through to the verdant backyard beyond. At the center of the residence, the living room is anchored by a gas fireplace and opens to a covered, heated patio designed for year-round entertaining. Additional spaces include a separate family room, a refined lounge with a fully equipped wet bar and ice maker, and a versatile loft.

The gourmet kitchen features an expansive seated island, premium appliances including a built-in coffee bar, and a butler's pantry with temperature-controlled wine storage. Five generously sized en-suite bedrooms provide exceptional comfort and privacy, including a primary suite with a separate sitting room, brick fireplace, custom dressing room, and a spa-inspired bath. Outside, a true California oasis awaits — pool and spa, fire pit, sport court, outdoor kitchen, and sprawling lawn — moments from The Riviera Country Club, the beaches of the Pacific Coast, and Brentwood.
