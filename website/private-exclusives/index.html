Off-Market FAQ

Frequently asked.

What is a Compass Private Exclusive?

A Compass Private Exclusive is a home that is genuinely for sale but marketed only inside the Compass agent network — never published on the MLS, Zillow, Redfin, or other public sites. Sellers use them to protect privacy, test pricing, or transact discreetly, and buyers gain access through a Compass agent.

How does the weekly off-market report work?

Tell us your target neighborhoods and search criteria and we send you a curated report of the off-market and pre-MLS homes that match — typically 30 to 90 days before they would reach the public market. It's not a daily firehose; it arrives when there's something worth your time.

Is there a cost to get access?

No. You get one month of free access to start, and you keep it going only if it's useful — cancel anytime. Buyer-side commissions are negotiated as part of any transaction and are typically paid through the listing, so there's no fee to join the report.

How are these homes different from what I see on Zillow?

Roughly one in three luxury LA transactions has an off-market component, so if you're only watching Zillow and Redfin you're seeing about two-thirds of the market. The report surfaces the Compass Private Exclusives and pocket listings that never hit those public sites, matched to your search.
