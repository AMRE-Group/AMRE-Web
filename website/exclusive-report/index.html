FAQ

Common questions.

What is a Compass Private Exclusive?

A listing marketed privately within the Compass network before — or instead of — appearing on the MLS and public portals like Zillow. Sellers use them for privacy or to test pricing; buyers who see them get first access.

Is the weekly report really free?

Yes. There's no fee and no obligation — it's how we meet serious buyers. Unsubscribe anytime.

How is this different from a Zillow alert?

Zillow can only alert you to public listings. This report covers homes that aren't public yet — plus cross-brokerage pocket listings we hear about through our network.

Do I need to be pre-approved to receive it?

No. But when a home fits, we can connect you with trusted lenders so you're ready to move quickly.
